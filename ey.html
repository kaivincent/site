Ernst & Young

In spring 2023, our Industry Practicum class at the USC Iovine and Young Academy partnered with the Health consultancy at Ernst & Young (EY) to "reimagine care". In particular, we were asked:

How might emerging technologies and other societal trends reimagine experiences of ‘care’ in the future?

Our process involved:

  • Identifying Health Trends
  • Investigating Emerging Technologies
  • Conducting User Research
  • Envisioning Future Scenarios
  • Developing Seed Concepts
  • Building a Prototype and Telling the Story

Our Proposal

Our team proposed Nora, The Friendly Robot Nurse. Nora is an intelligent public healthcare robot that patrols neighborhoods and populous areas to provide personalized care through easy access to medical equipment and vital health scans.
